ABOUT US

Top Interior Designers in Kenya

Cherry Interior is a premier interior design company in Kenya, renowned for its comprehensive approach that encompasses both top-notch services and an extensive range of high-quality products.

With a rich history and a devoted client base, it has solidified its position as an industry leader, representing distinguished international brands like Germany’s Hettich Group, Hepo, South Africa’s Sonae Arauco, and Korea’s LG Boards. Embodying excellence, Cherry Interior excels in delivering exceptional design services while serving as a pivotal distributor of premium interior products across Africa.

Cherry Interior is your perfect partner for residential, hospitality, or commercial spaces.

We manufacture and sell original quality dry timber and wood products such as kitchens, wardrobes, and furniture, all made from mahogany, mvule, and cypress, as well as block boards, MDF boards, and particle boards.
